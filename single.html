Inside A Mind Of Professional Tennis Player

Everyone in their life have been in a critical situation where they made a lot of decisions and had many different ideas in a short time. But have you ever wondered what happens inside a professional player’s mind during intense points that keeps their confidence and success happening.

For solution we have interviewed current World’s 5th best player from Spain – Alvaro Rodrigez. First of all he reveals that mindset is the key to success in tennis because mood controls the quality of personal performance. Alvaro says that most of the time he tries to think about positive things like previous winnings or positive words that were said by people around him. As well he reveals that during breaks, in his mind he goes to calm dream place where he can relax and ran away from stress to help concentrate on upcoming points. Finally, player says that during the point a lot of times he automatically thinks about possibilities and results of each his decision and opponent’s actions which most of times helps, but sometimes becomes disturbing and can drive crazy.

All in all Alvaro Rodrigez is amazed how his mind processes so many things in such a short time without him intending to do so and says that these skills make such things as being successful in critical situations.
